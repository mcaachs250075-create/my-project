Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I water indoor plants?

Most indoor plants need watering once or twice a week depending on the season.

Do all plants need direct sunlight?

No. Some plants prefer indirect sunlight or partial shade.

When should I use fertilizer?

Apply organic fertilizer once every month during the growing season.
